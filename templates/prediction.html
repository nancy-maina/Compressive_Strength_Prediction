The strength of the concrete is measured in MPa (Megapascals)


The strength of the concrete is calculated using the following formula

Strength = 2.12 * (cement + blast_furnace_slag + fly_ash + water + superplasticizer + coarse_aggregate + fine_aggregate) + 0.001 * age
